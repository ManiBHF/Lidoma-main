Discover Lidoma's Gaming

Levelup

PLAY :

LIDOMA, with its mission to bolster the gaming industry, has commenced its operations to host a variety of equitable tournaments. Play not just for the thrill, but also for the camaraderie and spirit of competition

EARN :

While participating in these impartial tournaments, players can also tap into our expansive gaming database. This allows them to join competitive events either solo or with friends, providing ample opportunities to play and secure cash rewards. Earn as you immerse yourself in a world of strategy, reflexes, and sheer fun.

ENJOY :

Our extensive gaming database is not just a repository; it's a treasure trove for gamers. Featuring a myriad of games across all genres to suit your preferences, it's tailored for every kind of gamer out there. Whether you're squeezing in a quick game during your break or settling in for a marathon session, with LIDOMA, you can play, earn, and most importantly, enjoy every moment of it.

About Lidoma

Lidoma Vision Esports, a UAE-based International Gaming & Esports Solutions Company established in 2020, aims to empower the global gaming community & ecosystem. Our passionate team of gaming enthusiasts leads us on an exciting journey in the worldwide gaming landscape. At Lidoma, we Inspire the World to Play, Earn, and Enjoy. We unite communities, orchestrate tournaments & leagues, and broadcast online/offline events to create a robust platform that offers extensive services to our partners & community.
