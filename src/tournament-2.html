About Lidoma

Lidoma Vision Esports, a UAE-based International Gaming & Esports Solutions Company established in 2020, aims to empower the global gaming community & ecosystem. Our passionate team of gaming enthusiasts leads us on an exciting journey in the worldwide gaming landscape. At Lidoma, we Inspire the World to Play, Earn, and Enjoy. We unite communities, orchestrate tournaments & leagues, and broadcast online/offline events to create a robust platform that offers extensive services to our partners & community.
